ABOUT KAYA SENIOR SECONDARY SCHOOL

Kaya Senior Secondary School is a multi-religious School which does not discriminate against other religious denominations.

We have a well trained professional staff team always ready,committed and determined to provide good quality education to our students.

At Kaya Senior Secondary School,the power is always on and we also have a standby generator to ensure that the school is always powered for our students revision benefit.

The School also has a constant water supply and enough water reservours to ensure that cleanliness and sanitation are maintained they are our number one priority because a good health is wealth.

The School is also enclosed in a fence to ensure that outsiders do not encroach in the School yard or even steal our student's and School properties

At Kaya Senior Secondary School,security is maximum and we have two security personels and nobody enters without signing in the book at the gate to be able to track down any one who may happen to do wrong within School campus or to our students within.

We have a school bus which transports our students whenever they is a need to like for research tours and seminars and many other occasions

At Kaya Senior Secondary School we have morder sportsuch as a foot ball pitch,basket ball court,volley ball court and many others

Kaya Senior Secondary School was started in 1995 and it is a mixed day and boarding school.

So many activies happen at our School which benefit our students in their learning process,educationally,morally and spiritually.

At Kaya SSS we do not not only concentrate on theory classes but practical as well and hand skills too.
