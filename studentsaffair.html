STUDENTS WELFARE

At Kaya Senior Secondary School we accomodate both boys and girls because it is a mixed School and we have dormitories for both genders.

The School dormitories are big enough to accomodate all our students and we are constructing other premises to cater for more students since we expect the number to be rising each academic year due to our excellent performance

All our premises are in installed in with fire extinguishers to cater for the emergency of fire out break incase it happens

For the boys they have their Patron and girls their matron who address their school and private issues who also foward them to the School administration if need be.

The Patron and matron are responsible for monitoring the student's behaviours, keep money for the young students who can not do it for them selves,monitor the student's behaviours and also maintain law and order among them.

All our dormitories are supplied with enough water and power with a standby generator incase of power blackout.
