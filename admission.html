ADMISSION PROCESS FOR NEW STUDENTS
Admission requirements vary slightly according to the class and level of education the applicant is seeking admission for and or individual circumstances.The level in which the applicant wishes to register may prescribe certain additional requirements if the student is deemed to be deficient.

Students who wish to join senior one and senior five have to be with grades ranging from a 1st and a 3rd grade.
Students who wish to join in other classes have to present report cards or promotion academic information from their former schools.

Application procedure

Obtain an application form from the director's office

Fill the form correctly and attach copies of all your education certificates and documents along with the application fee as stipulated in the application form.

Return these documents promptly to the directior's office atleast 3 weeks before the beginning of the term.

If your application is accepted,you will recieve a notification of acceptance letter from the director.You may come to the school only if you have received the acceptance letter

Academic documents for Admission

At the time of the student's registration,the student is required to present original documents.They will be certified and returned to the student.However,a student may be requested to present them again at the director's office or the head master's office should the need arise.

Registration

Students are required to report to the school on the scheduled date so that they may register in time and begin classes in time.Usually,one week is set apart for registration.During registration,new students are given orientation about the school about the school and it's programs and facilities.

Registration Procedures


There are five basic steps in the registration procedures as follows:
At the cashier's office,students need to;

Arrrange financial payment

Obtain a registration form

Obtain financial clearance

Pay house club fee

Be assigned to a house club

At the Director's office student's need to;

Complete registration of student's details obtain registration cards printed from the database.Individual registration cards are necessary to gain entry when classes begin.

Keep the registration card safe for day to day use

Students who for any reason arrive after the closing date of registration may be allowed to register.However,a fee that increases daily is chaeged for late registration.

(Consult the financial section of this bulletin in the accounts office staff for details)

Late registration officially closes at the at the conscent of the deputy head master,however the school may consider registration of students with special cases such as death,accidents in the family but will not assign a full load.
