FINANCIAL INFORMATION

FEES
Kaya Senior Secondary School fees are competitive and determined by the number of features and services provided by the school such as boarding,library,medical and many others.
The fees structure is subject to change.However it can not change with in the academic year.
The total amount to be charged to every student is computed using the academic fees structure approved by the School council.
The copies of fees structures are available at the director's office and are given free of charge at your request.

Payments
All fees must be paid in any of the bank accounts of the school given below before registration.Bank charges for the other banks vary depending on their internal policies.
The deposit slip must be presented to the recieving cashier with in the month of the deposit

Postdated and Unpaid Cheques

 Postdated cheques are not acceptable.No student presenting a postdated cheque will be financially cleared.
Cases related to bounced cheques are to be treated in compliance with banks other government regulationsin place.

General Financial Matters

 If a student neglects or refuses to pay amounts owed to the school,the administrative board reserves the right to withhold any or all registration materials,student examination results/report until the student settles the amount with the school.
Such fees include tuition,library fees,damage costs and many others.Incase of any and other legitmate charges
Breakage or Damage:Students are held responsible for the cost of any breakage or damage which they may cause.
Personal Effects:Students shoul ensure that personal effects are covered by insurance.
